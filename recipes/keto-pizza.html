Keto Pizza

Love pizza but you don't want the carbs try this recipe. It will keep you in ketosis and allow you to enjoy the food you love

Yield:8 Servings Prep Time:15 min Cook Time:15 - 20 min Total Time:35 min

Nutrition Info(Crust Only):

Per Serving:167 Cal | 12.4g Fat | 3.2g Carbs | 1.2g Fiber | 0.9g Sugar | 8.6g Protein

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cups Almond Flour
  • 1 3/4 cups Krafts Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
  • 2 Tbsp(1 oz.) Cream Chesse
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 tsp Italian Seasoning
  • Desired Toppings(tomato/sugar-free bbq sauce, mozzarella cheese, pepperoni, salami, chicken, bell peppers)

Directions:

  1. Pre-heat oven to 425Â°F.
  2. Combine the almond flour and mozzarella cheese in a microwave safe bowl. Mix in cheese cheese, then cook in the microwave for one minute. Stir and cook for an additional 30 sec in the microwave. Repeat last part until fully mixed.
  3. Mix in egg and italian seasoning.
  4. Shape dough into a ball, place between two sheets of parchment paper.
  5. Roll the dough into a disired shape.
  6. Remove the top parchment paper, place dough onto a baking/pizza pan. Place in oven for 12-14 minutes remove from oven once the crust is slightly brown.
  7. Add toppings: start with a layer of sauce, the layer on other toppings
  8. Return pizza to oven for another 5 minutes until cheese has melted
  9. Enjoy
