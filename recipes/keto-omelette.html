Simple American Style Omelette

Making an American style omelette is all about achieving a golden, slightly textured exterior with a fully set(not dry) interior. Unlike the french omelette, the american omelette is hearty and usually folded in half.

Yield:1 Serving Prep Time:5-10 mins Cook Time:3-5 min Total Time:8-15 min

Nutrition Info:

Per Serving:255-325 Cal | 21-26 Fat | 10-12g Sat. Fat | 1.5-2g Carbs | 13-19g Protein | Cholesterol 395-580mg | 160-230mg Sodium

Ingredients:

  • 2-3 Large Eggs
  • 1 Tbsp Heavy Whipping Cream
  • 1 Tbsp Butter
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Fillings: Shredded cheese, bacon, diced ham, bell peppers, diced onions,...

Directions:

  1. Prep eggs: In a small bowl, break the york of each egg with a fork, lightly wisk eggs
  2. Heat med(8") skillet: Place skillet over med-high heat, add butter, swirl around until foam has subsides/sizzling, and butter has completely melted but not browned.
  3. The magic: Pour eggs into the center of the skillet. Let set until for a few seconds until edges have set. Gently push the edges towards the center while tilting the pan allowing the raw eggs to fill the empty spaces.
  4. Add filling: Once the top is mostly set, add toppings to one-half of the omelette.
  5. Fold it: Slide a spatula under the unfilled side and fold it over the fillings.
  6. Finally: Let sit for another ~30 secs to melt any cheese and give the bottom the signature golden brown "American-style" finish.
  7. ENJOY!

Tips:

  • DO NOT! over wisk eggs, adding a massive amount of air will cause the omelette to be rubbery.
  • Saute any veggies before hand.
  • Sprikle a few toppings on the finish product for presentation.
